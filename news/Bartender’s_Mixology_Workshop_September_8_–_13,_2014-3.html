Bartender’s Mixology Workshop September 8 – 13, 2014

September 23, 2014
Windstar Cruises BLD Stewards Noel Isaac, Sammy Lachica, Kennedy Latosa, Rhone Soriano, Ivy Red Mendoza and GPA Deckboys Sammy Fernandez and Eduardo Chan successfully completed the Bartenders’ Mixology Workshop under the tutelage of a team of trainers from the Philippine Bartender’s League headed by National President Sir Mutley Matilla. Good luck to the graduates and happy mixing and serving onboard.
