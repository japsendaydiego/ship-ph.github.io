Flambé

Out of the ordinary! No need to be a master chef! A Delicious Alternative to Team Building

Flambé (French) means to ignite foods that have liqueur added to it. This is done for a dramatic effect, to develop a rich flavor of the liqueur to the food without adding the alcohol.

SHIP (Ship Hotel Cruise Institute of the Phils, Inc.) offers a unique twist to the corporate team building experience. An entirely different take on the usual obstacle-course or treasure-hunt type of activity, it can build unity, bring out hidden talents often unseen in the work place and break down communication barriers that could lead to a happier and more productive team.

Flambé can be run for groups of up to 30 people although more can join by arrangement. You can choose from Asian, Western, and Mediterranean cuisines.

Sometimes before the group can work well together inside the office, they need to learn how to work well together outside of it. What better way to lighten the mood than by doing it the Flambé way.

Rev up your team building exercise with this one of a kind culinary experience!

Let the games begin!
