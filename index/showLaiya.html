Laiya Coco Grove

Laiya Coco Grove is a resort at Aplaya Laiya, San Juan, Batangas where SHIP serves as its partner in providing training for resort staff in the areas of F&B Service, Housekeeping and Food production and also as its partner in providing cakes, breads and desserts for wedding functions and other events. LCG also serves as an on-the-job training venue for the students of SHIP.
