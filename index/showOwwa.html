OWWA SCHOLARSHIP

A qualified applicant may avail of Technical – vocational course being offered under the SESP, following the regular school year calendar, offered to landlbased or seabased OFWd or their beneficiaries/dependents. Upon receipt of the scholarship voucherm the grantee may enroll in any TESDA/DOST/MTC –registered program and other government institutions. Program choices shall be supportive of the land and sea based skills requirement.

Financial Assistance

The financial assistance per scholar is P14,500. The amount shall be paid directly to the TESDA/DOST/MTC –accredited training institution as tuition fee. If the tuition is more than the financial assistance, the scholar shall shoulder the difference.
