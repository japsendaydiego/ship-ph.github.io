The Commodore’s Table

The Commodore’s Table started as the practical training venue for SHIP trainees and had since expanded into a full service restaurant that offers breakfast, lunch and snacks to offices in the building and to walk-in guests. Through the years, it had slowly added new services such as lunch boxes delivery, outside catering, in-house catering and social functions like birthdays and anniversaries. The Commodore’s Table provides dining services to corporate events at SHIP such as seminars, board meetings, conferences, workshops and trainings. Included in the product list of The Commodore’s Table are cookies, bars, cupcakes, breads, pastries and wedding cakes.
